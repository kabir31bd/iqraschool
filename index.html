WELCOME TO INDIA'S BEST VALUE BASED EDUCATION SCHOOL

IQRA International School is India's best value based Montessori and Primary School. We cater to each child's social, emotional and developmental needs providing an environment which allows each child to grow with self confidence, fully equipped to become a balanced successful human being in this world and the hereafter.

More than just a child enrichment centre, the IQRA experience is a Total Learning Experience for young children! The IQRA Teaching Philosophy encompasses the psychology behind the Montessori method, the integration of Islamic values as our guiding principles in life, and the English Language as our medium of instruction and primary language of communication.
